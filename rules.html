Rules of the Guessing Game

  • The game will generate a random number between 1 and 100.
  • Your goal is to guess the number.
  • If your guess is too high, you'll get a hint: "Too high!".
  • If your guess is too low, you'll get a hint: "Too low!".
  • Keep guessing until you find the correct number!
  • Challenge yourself to guess in as few tries as possible.
